READ CASE STUDY

Migration, Accessibility, ACTION!

Introduction: Intellium was contacted by an Emmy Award-nominated small business film studio that wanted to migrate and update collaboration platforms to increase performance and security.

The Challenge: Like many businesses, the company was on an older email platform provided by their web host. They were using IMAP, but not all devices could access email services. They also lacked basic recommended security controls such as two-factor authentication (2FA). Additionally,the email system they had did not support DKIM to ensure message integrity.

How could they implement a comprehensive solution that would allow them to communicate, plan and collaborate effectively, be easy to use, and enhance security for their business?

Solution: Most of the company already had personal email accounts with Gmail, so Intellium recommended the Google Workspace solution, which would minimize their learning curve with a new platform, migrate existing email conveniently, offer and enforce improved security policies, and provide great ease of use for company employees. Working around the studio’s schedule, Intellium planned for a weekend migration that would transfer the company to an improved system with very little downtime.

Results:

  • Secure, convenient mobile access to their collaboration suite via nearly any medium (webmail, smart phone, tablets, email clients on computers, etc).
  • Web domain security against spoofing, and lower spam scores with properly configured SPF, DKIM, and DMARC
  • Increased email deliverability
  • Reduced chance of account compromise with implementation of two-factor authentication (2FA) possibly by up to 99.9% (source: Microsoft)
  • Improved spam protection keeping inboxes tidy and reducing time wasted deleting spam
  • Convenient new collaboration tools for everything from scheduling meetings with clients to securely exchanging emails and files

Conclusion: Intellium successfully migrated and improved the studio’s business IT, conveniently moving them a modern, trusted, high security and easy-to-use set of solutions for every aspect of their business.
